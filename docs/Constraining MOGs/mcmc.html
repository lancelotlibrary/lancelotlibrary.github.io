Markov Chain Monte Carlo

In order to perform the N-body simulations for modified cosmologies, we firstly need to constrain every single one of them with the use of various cosmological datasets to determine observables like Ωm0\Omega_{m0}Ωm0​ or H0H_0H0​ and derive the Hubble rate as well as effective gravitational constant GeffG_{\rm eff}Geff​. In our case, we have used Observational Hubble Data (OHD), Supernovae type-Ia distance modulus, Baryon Acoustic Oscillations (BAO) and Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) datasets to constrain our models.
